Problem and Solution

Inspired by my own clumsiness, I created a caf√© that celebrates this trait. To reflect this, I designed unique stickers that resonate with people who tend to be clumsy. Not to exclude the idea that clumsy people can, at the same time, be elegant and organized, I balanced it all out by pairing a white background with a black sans-serif font. Spill offers a welcoming space for everyone to enjoy and appreciate good coffee.

Fun Fact

The chosen font features ink traps, symbolizing the pouring of coffee when it spills
