Spill

Project type

Visual identity & packaging

Purpose

University project

Software

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom

Year

2024

Theme

Spill Café embodies a cozy and inviting vibe, ideal for work or leisure. Balancing professionalism with warmth, it serves high-quality coffee to a diverse clientele, including both casual drinkers and connoisseurs. With its unique charm as a café for the clumsy, Spill offers a welcoming space for everyone to enjoy and appreciate good coffee.

Problem and solution

The idea was to create a visual identity with character. Given that the café's name is Spill, evoking the notion of clumsily spilling coffee, I needed to represent that characteristic while maintaining a professional appearance of the visual identity. For this reason, I opted for an extended typeface with inktraps. To infuse personality, I decided to design quirky stickers.

Strategy

Because Spill wants to attract customers with different personalities, creating a design that appeals to everyone was tough. The main plan was to keep the design simple. A professional look is established by pairing white background with a black sans-serif font, which is then enhanced with unique stickers.

Process

To add personality to the brand, the customization of the typeface was necessary. This customization aimed to infuse the brand with a unique identity, enhancing its visual appeal and distinctiveness in the market.
