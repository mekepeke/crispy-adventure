Manus manum lavat

Project type

Risograph Posters

Purpose

Workshop project

Software

Paper, scanner, risograph

Year

2024

Theme

In the International Design Week Workshop, we had to address important political problems. Due to recent events (elections) in Croatia, I chose an ongoing problem: government corruption.

Problem and solution

To highlight this issue ironically, I used the well-known saying “Manus manum lavat” (translated to Croatian as “Ruka ruku mije”). The concept was to illustrate how government officials consistently protect each other when money is involved, ensuring mutual benefits from their actions.

Strategy

My approach was to use a recognizable face paired with a compelling layout. For instance, hands encircling a head symbolize how individuals will always safeguard those who benefit them, even through illicit means. Additionally, the arrangement of the hands and head resembles a virus, representing a societal disease.

Process

The project was developed using analog techniques. I used printed images relevant to my topic, along with some supplied images, which restricted my selection. I created three layers that we scanned and printed using a risograph. Finally, I used an AxiDraw to produce handwritten text for the project.
