Kinder Filmfestival Redesign

Project Type

Visual Identity

Purpose

University/Client Project

Software

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, Figma

Year

2025

Problem and Solution

The task was to redesign the outdated visual identity of the festival while preserving its core characteristics, such as playfulness, and incorporating new qualities like openness and uniqueness. To achieve this, a design was created that ensures the festival stands out and captures attention. Bold colors and dynamic gradients were employed to highlight the festival's four main values: community, creativity, education and experience.
