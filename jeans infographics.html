What about jeans?

Project Type

Infographics Brochure

Purpose

University Project - Creation and Conception

Mentor

Christoph Almasy

Software

Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop

Year

2025

Problem and Solution

The task for this course was to create infographics on any topic. I chose jeans because they are, in my opinion, one of the most popular clothing items ever made. To align with the theme, I made several design choices. The brochure's long, narrow format symbolizes the shape of jeans, and I used the Denim Ink font from Displaay Type Foundry for its connection to the topic. I selected risography printing for its distinctive blue tone, representing denim, and used blue thread for binding to reinforce the denim theme.

Fun Fact

The images are scans of my second-hand jeans, the inspiration for this project.
