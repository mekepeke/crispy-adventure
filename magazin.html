Who am I?

Project type

Magazine

Purpose

University project

Software

Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom

Year

2024

Theme

A magazine centered around a random object chosen by each student from a thrift shop. In my case it was a metallic apple jar.

Problem and solution

The main task of the course was to create a magazine that captures the atmosphere of the selected object. For my object, I chose 3 adjectives (mysterious, reserved, and eye-catching) which served as main starting point for the magazine's design.

Strategy

The magazine transitions from structured to chaotic, mirroring the journey of the apple's inner state. Beginning with a classic layout, the magazine gradually breaks free, with compositions becoming less recognizable and text unreadable. This symbolizes the apple's undefined and unpredictable nature, exploring the question "Who am I?". Posters towards the end of the magazine further reflect this theme, with the apple ultimately breaking free from its boundaries and exiting the magazine.

Process

In this assignment, I am the author of all the used elements (text, photographs, illustrations, experimental typography). This required a lot of planning. The most challenging part was sifting through a pile of documents to select a few that best represented my subject and arranging them in the magazine to tell the story effectively.
