Print Production Booklet

Project type

Booklet

Purpose

University project

Software

Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop

Year

2024

Theme

Creating an engaging and practical booklet for the final exam of the Print Production course.

Problem and solution

The challenge was to design a booklet that is both captivating and functional for learning purposes. To address this, I created a product that is interactive and enjoyable to use. I selected pink paper with dark blue text to add a fun and visually appealing element.

Strategy

To deviate from traditional binding methods, I employed a plastic band to hold the pages together, forming a loose-leaf system. This approach allows each page to be handled individually, making it easier to study specific sections without carrying the entire booklet. This flexibility enhances the learning experience by allowing the user to focus on one page at a time.

Process

The booklet was designed using InDesign and printed at the university, requiring familiarity with various printing techniques and guidelines. To protect the booklet from damage, I created a custom folded map to house the loose pages securely.
