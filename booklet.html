Print Production Booklet

Project Type

Booklet

Purpose

University Project

Software

Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop

Year

2024

Problem and Solution

The idea was to design an engaging and functional booklet for studying in the Print Production course. I decided to create the booklet in the form of small flashcards, each featuring one important sentence we learned in the course. The goal of this booklet is for the user to 'draw' one paper at a time and learn sentence by sentence. This approach makes the booklet more engaging and interesting.

Fun Fact

To hold the pages together I used a plastic band, forming a loose-leaf system. Technically, it is still considered a binding technique, but I actually used it because I didn't want to do something too complicated and it was also an interesting way to try something new.
