Laconiq

Project type

Visual identity

Purpose

Client project

Software

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom, Adobe After Effects, Adobe InDesign

Year

2023

Theme

Laconiq is a new digital marketing agency whose motto is "short and clear". The main characteristics of Laconiq are direct communication and the use of new technologies. I chose to create a modern and minimalistic logo by prioritizing clear communication and advanced technologies. This logo features a simple geometric typeface and an icon formed from the letters.

Problem and solution

The client wanted the company to be perceived as approachable, bright, creative, and engaging. Recognizing that many creative agencies adopt a very serious approach in their work, we decided to create a visual identity that is playful yet also reflects the agency's consistency. We developed an icon that can be utilized to narrate various creative stories unfolding within the agency.

Strategy

To ensure consistency, a mascot was derived from the lettermark. The icon is easily animatable and was designed to infuse playfulness and intrigue into the brand. A broad color palette is employed to highlight the diversity of the digital agency while simultaneously attracting clients who seek similar atmosphere for their projects. Considerable thought was devoted to crafting the Instagram grid's appearance, as a strong presence on Instagram is the primary strategy for attracting young clients with diverse perspectives.

Process

The most challenging aspect of the project was finding the right balance between playfulness and seriousness. We didn't want the logo to appear overly playful, as it might convey a sense of irresponsibility from the company's side. However, we also didn't want it to look too serious, considering the aim was to attract clients seeking a change in their online presence.
