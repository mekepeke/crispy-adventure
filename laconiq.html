Problem and Solution

Laconiq is a new digital marketing agency with a simple motto: "short and clear." The agency focuses on straightforward communication and staying on top of new technologies. To stand out from the usual crowd, we decided to give the brand a fun twist by designing a playful icon and using a wide, colorful palette to bring a fresh, energetic vibe. Laconiq aims to be different-modern, approachable, and just a bit more fun than an average agency.
