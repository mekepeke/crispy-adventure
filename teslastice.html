Tesslastice

Project type

Visual identity

Purpose

personal project

Software

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom

Year

2022

Theme

A patisserie visual identity that presents itself as playful, fun, and creative. Because 18th birthday cakes are always a good idea, I wanted this brand to stand out and appeal to a younger audience, especially Gen Z and Millenials.

Problem and solution

Design of a visual identity that stands out from the competition. After researching the market, I decided that the primary visual identity should not be white, but rather in a dark mode. To ensure the identity doesn't appear too off-putting or serious, I preserved playfulness by adding interesting icons that represent cake slicing. These icons create a very intriguing pattern that is then integrated throughout the entire visual identity.

Strategy

The target audience are people between 18-40 years. To effectively reach them, the brand has a presence on Facebook and Instagram. Secondary logos, suitable for social media profiles, were created. Using stickers, patterns, and images in posts boosts engagement and brand connection. Consistent use of these elements across platforms aims to create a cohesive brand experience

Process

The pivotal aspect of the process in this project was brainstorming ideas since I aimed to ensure that the final design wouldn't appear overly simplistic, dull, or lacking in thoughtfulness.
