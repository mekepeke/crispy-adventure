Advent

Project type

Posters

Purpose

Personal project

Software

Blender, Adobe Illustrator

Year

2022

Theme

The Christmas Advent in Zagreb stands out as one of the most renowed in Europe. Each Christmas season revives the essence of traditional culture, flavors, sophistication, and spiritual richness.

Problem and solution

I've chosen to create 3D representations of Zagreb's key locations hosting the Christmas markets (such as Ban Jelacic Square, King Tomislav Square, and Zrinjevac). These models were then transformed into baubles, symbolizing the link between Zagreb and the festive spirit of Christmas.

Strategy

My approach to these posters involves making the modeled spheres visually captivating. To sustain the interest, I decided to create reflections of light on the baubles, guiding the observer's gaze around the 3D models. Concealing the text behind the baubles aims to spark curiosity and evoke a subtle sense of the mystique associated with Christmas.

Process

The most important part was creating 3D models. I had to decide which building features were critical to keep, and which could be removed while keeping the solution recognizable.
