Advent

Project Type

Posters

Purpose

Personal Project

Software

Blender, Adobe Illustrator

Year

2022

Problem and Solution

The Christmas Advent in Zagreb stands out as one of the most renowed in Europe. Each Christmas season revives the essence of traditional culture, flavors, sophistication, and spiritual richness. I've chosen to create 3D representations of Zagreb's key locations hosting the Christmas markets. These models were then transformed into baubles, symbolizing the link between Zagreb and the festive spirit of Christmas.
