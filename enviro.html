Enviro

Project type

Visual identity

Purpose

Personal project

Software

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom

Year

2023

Theme

Enviro is a straightforward zero-waste food retailer that does not fall for other peopleâ€™s justifications for non-eco-friendly behaviour.

Problem and solution

The primary objective includes creating a visual identity that is not aggressive. The brand's goal is not to shame people if they don't think in an eco-friendly way, but to encourage them to consider the consequences of their actions. For this reason, I approached the brand in a funky manner. As a result, vibrant and bold colors are utilized, which are also linked to the hues of the Earth.

Strategy

The concept aims for the brand to be visually striking and lively, which is achieved through the incorporation of vibrant green and blue hues. Elements derived from the logo form a dynamic wave pattern, injecting a sense of fun. To balance its playful appeal with a sense of seriousness, powerful text is integrated alongside the main logo to promote awareness. Additionally, clear and straightforward imagery is used to prevent design saturation.

Process

Throughout this project, a key focus was dedicated to typeface selection, as it shapes the brand's overall ambiance, ultimately inspiring the creation of the pattern.
