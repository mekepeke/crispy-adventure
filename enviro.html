Enviro

Project Type

Visual Identity

Purpose

Personal Project

Software

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Lightroom

Year

2023

Problem and Solution

This project was developed for an Instagram challenge, where the brief was to create a straightforward, zero-waste food retailer. I added a touch of sass to the brand, which is reflected in the motto, “0 Bullshit.” The aim of Enviro is to inspire people to think critically about the consequences of their actions.
